About Me

My name is Dakota Blanchard, I'm 24 and I have been making and recording music for as long as I can remember. I have worked out of multiple recording studios throughout the years and have helped build several of them. I held an internship at Getaway Recordings in Haverhill, MA for a little over a year as a tracking assistant. Below you can find examples of recent audio work that I have done.
